## AI & Machine Learning

### DeepSeek Unveils New Flagship Open-Source Model
- **Source:** Bloomberg
- **URL:** https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-24/deepseek-unveils-newest-flagship-a-year-after-ai-breakthrough
- **Summary:** DeepSeek rolled out preview versions of a new flagship AI model, calling it the most powerful open-source platform on the market — a year after the firm first upended Silicon Valley. The release directly targets OpenAI and Anthropic on both capability and price.
- **Impact:** Reinforces the structural pressure open-source frontier models put on closed-API margins. Builders evaluating model spend should re-benchmark; investors should expect renewed compression of AI inference pricing through Q2.

### Google Deepens Thinking Machines Lab Partnership
- **Source:** TechCrunch
- **URL:** https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/22/exclusive-google-deepens-thinking-machines-lab-ties-with-new-multi-billion-dollar-deal/

### Yann LeCun's AMI Labs Raises $1B Seed at $3.5B Valuation
- **Source:** Crescendo AI / industry reports
- **URL:** https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates
- **Summary:** Advanced Machine Intelligence Labs, founded by Meta's outgoing chief AI scientist Yann LeCun, raised $1.03 billion in seed funding at a $3.5 billion valuation. The lab is building "world models" — an alternative architecture to LLMs that learns by simulating physical reality.

- **Source:** Distill Intelligence
- **URL:** https://www.distillintelligence.com/briefings/semiconductors-ai-chips-2026-04-24

## Tech Policy & Regulation

### White House National AI Policy Framework Pushes State Preemption
- **Source:** Consumer Finance Monitor
- **URL:** https://www.consumerfinancemonitor.com/2026/04/08/the-white-houses-national-policy-framework-for-artificial-intelligence-what-it-means-and-what-comes-next/
- **Summary:** The White House released a National Policy Framework for AI in March recommending federal preemption of state AI laws, child-privacy and age-verification rules for AI services, and protections against unauthorized AI digital replicas of voice and likeness.
- **Impact:** Federal preemption would meaningfully reduce compliance burden for AI deployers currently navigating 50-state patchwork rules. Expect intense lobbying through Q3 — California and Texas attorneys general have already signaled opposition.

### State AI Bills Cross 600 in 2026 Sessions
- **Source:** Inside Global Tech
- **URL:** https://www.insideglobaltech.com/2026/04/06/u-s-tech-legislative-regulatory-update-first-quarter-2026/
- **Summary:** State legislators have introduced more than 600 AI bills targeting private entities so far in 2026. Indiana, Utah, and Washington enacted laws prohibiting health insurers from using AI as the sole basis for claim denials.
- **Impact:** Health-insurance AI vendors face the most immediate compliance lift. The broader 600-bill backdrop is precisely what the federal preemption push is meant to neutralize — until then, multi-state operators should budget for fragmented rollout.

### SELF DRIVE Act of 2026 Advances Toward Federal AV Framework
- **Source:** Global Policy Watch
- **URL:** https://www.globalpolicywatch.com/2026/04/u-s-tech-legislative-regulatory-update-first-quarter-2026/
- **Summary:** The House Energy and Commerce subcommittee held hearings on the SELF DRIVE Act of 2026 (H.R. 7390), which would establish a federal framework for autonomous vehicle deployment, replacing the current state-by-state approval mosaic.
- **Impact:** Federal AV preemption would unlock national rollout for Waymo, Tesla FSD-Robotaxi, and Zoox. The legislation's progress is the single highest-leverage near-term catalyst for AV equity valuations.
