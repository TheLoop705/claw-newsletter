## AI & Machine Learning

### Microsoft Launches Copilot Cowork with Anthropic's Claude Integration
- **Source:** Microsoft, The Verge
- **URL:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/blog/2026/03/30/copilot-cowork-now-available-in-frontier/
- **Summary:** Microsoft launched Copilot Cowork through its Frontier Program, bringing Anthropic's Claude into the Microsoft 365 ecosystem for long-running, multi-step tasks. The feature includes a Researcher agent for information gathering and a new Critique feature that has GPT draft research while Claude provides an edit pass for accuracy. This marks a significant collaboration between Microsoft and Anthropic within the enterprise AI space.

### China's AI Race Enters Industry-Specific Phase
- **Source:** CNBC
- **URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/cnbcs-china-connection-newsletter-ai-race-enters-a-new-phase.html
- **Summary:** Chinese companies are moving beyond general-purpose LLMs like DeepSeek toward industry-specific AI applications. Alibaba launched Accio Work, an AI-integrated sourcing platform that processes customs paperwork and calculates profit margins autonomously. Meanwhile, humanoid robot startups are focusing on specialized training data, with Linkerbot aiming to map all basic human hand skills within three years and produce robots cheaper than an iPhone.

### Apple Intelligence Accidentally Launches in China
- **Source:** The Verge, 9to5Mac
- **URL:** https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence
- **Summary:** Apple Intelligence features briefly appeared on iPhones in China before being taken offline. Bloomberg's Mark Gurman confirmed the launch was an "error." The Chinese government requires Apple to partner with local companies like Alibaba to power AI features in the country. Apple has been working to comply with local regulations while expanding its AI capabilities globally.

### Apple's Third-Party Siri Extensions Could Create an AI App Store
- **Source:** Bloomberg, The Verge
- **URL:** https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence
- **Summary:** Apple is reportedly opening Siri and Apple Intelligence to third-party services through an Extensions feature in iOS 27. These extensions will have a dedicated App Store section, effectively creating an AI marketplace. Users will be able to install third-party AI chatbots beyond ChatGPT and run them inside Siri, marking Apple's most ambitious AI platform play yet.

- **Source:** CNBC

### Google TurboQuant Continues to Rattle Memory Chip Sector
- **Source:** CNBC, Google Research
- **URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/google-ai-turboquant-memory-chip-stocks-samsung-micron.html
- **Summary:** The fallout from Google's TurboQuant compression breakthrough continues to pressure memory stocks. ## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran War Volatility Dominates:** Oil swings between $100-115/bbl as Trump oscillates between threatening Iran's infrastructure and signaling a willingness to exit. Markets remain hostage to headline risk, with the VIX above 30. Powell's reassurance of no rate hikes provided a floor for equities.

2. **AI Infrastructure Goes European:** Mistral's $830M data center financing, Nscale's $2B raise, and continued European capacity buildout signal that AI infrastructure is no longer a purely American story. European sovereignty concerns are driving investment.

3. **Memory Paradox:** Micron's 30% sell-off despite record demand illustrates the tension between AI's insatiable chip appetite and fears that efficiency breakthroughs (TurboQuant) could reduce demand. The Jevons paradox — where efficiency gains increase total consumption — likely applies here.

4. **India's Data Center Moment:** The Airtel/Nxtra $1B raise, combined with $50B+ commitments from Microsoft and Amazon, 20-year tax holidays, and projections of 5x capacity growth by 2030, position India as the next major data center hub.

5. **Quantum Computing's IPO Wave:** Multiple quantum firms are braving volatile markets to go public, betting that 18 months of scientific breakthroughs will translate into commercial momentum. Practical quantum advantage at ~100 logical qubits is expected by 2028-2029.

6. **Tech's Legal Reckoning:** Meta's dual court defeats on child safety signal a paradigm shift in how courts view Big Tech's liability for platform harms, potentially reshaping the entire social media business model.
