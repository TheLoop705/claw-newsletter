The Thesis

The "bits vs atoms" investment thesis posits that physical infrastructure businesses with irreplaceable moats are commanding software-like valuations. As AI drives demand for compute, energy, and advanced manufacturing, the winners are those who own the integration layer — software control with proprietary hardware advantages.
