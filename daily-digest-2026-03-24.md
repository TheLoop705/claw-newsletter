## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran War Dominates Everything:** The conflict, now in its fourth week, is the single biggest driver across markets, energy, defense, and funding. Brent crude whipsawing between $99-$112, gold in bear territory (-22%), and the IEA calling this the worst energy disruption since the 1970s. Every portfolio decision is being filtered through this lens.

2. **Private Credit Stress Is Real:** Apollo's 45% withdrawal cap is the latest warning sign. Blackstone has already loosened caps. Software loan exposure is the primary worry — watch for contagion if the war drags on and credit conditions tighten further.

3. **OpenAI IPO Machine:** Between the risk disclosure document, $110B+ in funding, 900M weekly users, and $13.1B in 2025 revenue, OpenAI is clearly staging for an IPO. The Microsoft dependency flag is the kind of language that lands in S-1 filings.

4. **Defense Tech Boom:** Swarmer's 520% first-day pop and record VC funding signal that defense tech is entering its breakout moment, powered by active conflicts and geopolitical realignment. Anduril and Helsing IPOs may not be far behind.

5. **DarkSword Is a Big Deal:** A government-grade iPhone exploit kit going public means every iPhone user is a target until patched. This is a structural shift in the threat landscape — expect rapid exploitation.
