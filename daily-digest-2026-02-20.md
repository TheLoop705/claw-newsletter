## AI & Machine Learning

### Global AI Spending Forecast to Hit $2.5 Trillion in 2026

## Tech Policy & Regulation

### EU Digital Services Act Marks Two Years
- **Source:** European Commission
- **URL:** https://commission.europa.eu/news-and-media/news/two-years-digital-services-act-ensuring-safer-online-spaces-2026-02-17_en
- **Summary:** The Digital Services Act (DSA) entered force in February 2024 and now applies to most platforms in the EU. The Commission has opened several investigations, including into X's deployment of Grok and Shein for addictive design. Large platforms face fines up to 6% of global annual turnover for non-compliance.

### UKRI Announces £1.6B AI Investment Framework
- **Source:** UKRI
- **URL:** https://www.ukri.org/publications/ukri-artificial-intelligence-research-and-innovation-strategic-framework/ukri-ai-research-and-innovation-strategic-framework/
- **Summary:** UK Research and Innovation announced over £1.6 billion directly targeted at the AI sector through 2031. The framework aims to position the UK as a global leader in explainable AI, agentic AI, edge computing, and sustainable models. Key goals include accelerating commercialization and building world-class AI infrastructure.

### Bank of England Proposes New Prudential Rules
- **Source:** Bank of England
- **URL:** https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/prudential-regulation/publication/2026/february/pra-rule-changes-to-accommodate-hmt-oprr-consultation-paper
- **Summary:** The PRA proposed amendments to accommodate HM Treasury's Overseas Prudential Requirements Regime (OPRR), affecting credit risk rules for banks and investment firms. Changes would become effective alongside Basel 3.1 package on January 1, 2027.

### Ireland Establishes Digital Regulatory Hub
- **Source:** Irish Government
- **URL:** https://www.gov.ie/en/department-of-the-taoiseach/publications/lead-digital-regulatory-hub-and-centre-of-expertise/

## Energy & Infrastructure

- **Source:** RBC Capital Markets
- **URL:** https://www.rbccm.com/en/insights/2026/02/global-power-demand-reshapes-infrastructure-investment-for-2026

### China's Clean Energy Boom Drives Economic Growth
- **Source:** Carbon Brief
- **URL:** https://www.carbonbrief.org/china-briefing-5-february-2026-clean-energys-share-of-economy-record-renewables-thawing-relations-with-uk/

This marks the first time wind and solar capacity has outranked coal capacity in China's power mix.

- **Source:** IISD
