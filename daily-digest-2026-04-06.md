### Drift Protocol Hacked for $285M in Largest DeFi Exploit of 2026
- **Source:** SharkStriker, BleepingComputer
- **URL:** https://sharkstriker.com/blog/april-2026-data-breaches/
- **Summary:** Attackers drained approximately $285 million from Solana-based decentralized exchange Drift Protocol on April 1. - **Summary:** The EU's Cybersecurity Service attributed the European Commission cloud hack to the TeamPCP threat group.
