### Amazon Wins Appeal Against Record €746M GDPR Fine
- **Source:** Reuters

## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran War Reshapes Everything:** The Strait of Hormuz crisis is the dominant macro driver — oil above $100/bbl, stocks at 2026 lows, central banks forced into rate hikes, and semiconductor supply chains under threat. Until the conflict de-escalates, every asset class is in its shadow.

2. **AI's Fiscal Reckoning:** Meta's 20% layoffs, Tesla's Terafab, and Morgan Stanley's breakthrough warning all point to the same truth — AI spending is massive and accelerating, but companies must now show returns or cut elsewhere. The "AI spend now, figure it out later" era is transitioning to "show me the money."

3. **Regulatory Whiplash:** The US withdrew its AI chip export rule while 78 state-level chatbot bills advance. Amazon's GDPR fine reversal weakens EU enforcement credibility. The regulatory landscape is fragmenting across jurisdictions, creating compliance headaches for global tech firms.

4. **Nuclear Renaissance:** The ARC Act ($3.6B), NRC reform under EO 14300, and the AI-driven energy demand surge are creating the strongest bipartisan push for nuclear power in decades. This is no longer theoretical — it's moving through Congress.

5. **VC Mega-Fund Supercycle:** With GC raising $10B, a16z at $15B, and February's record $189B in startup funding, the capital supply side is the most aggressive it's ever been. The concentration of capital into a few mega-funds raises questions about market distortion and valuation discipline.
