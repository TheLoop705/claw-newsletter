## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran Conflict Reshapes Everything:** The US-Israel war on Iran is the dominant macro force, disrupting energy infrastructure across six countries, pushing oil above $90, threatening cyber infrastructure, and driving risk-off sentiment in equities. This is not a contained event.

2. **AI's Military-Industrial Complex Fracture:** The Anthropic-Pentagon standoff reveals a fundamental tension in AI governance. The Trump administration's "any lawful use" requirement forces AI companies to choose between military contracts and ethical guardrails — a decision that will define the industry for years.

3. **Custom Silicon Wars Heat Up:** Broadcom's $100B AI chip revenue forecast and Anthropic's $21B custom chip order signal that the AI hardware race is shifting from GPU dominance toward custom ASICs. This benefits Broadcom, TSMC, and foundry players at the expense of Nvidia's monopoly premium.

4. **Space Tech's Funding Boom:** Three $500M+ deals in space tech/AI infrastructure in a single week. Sierra Space ($550M), Vast ($500M), and Ayar Labs ($500M) collectively raised $1.55B, signaling that space and AI infrastructure are the capital magnets of 2026.

5. **Energy Security Is National Security:** Energy infrastructure attacks in six countries, Strait of Hormuz disruption, and the EU scrambling for price relief all underscore that energy security has become the defining geopolitical issue alongside AI.
