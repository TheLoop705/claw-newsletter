## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran War Reshapes Everything:** The Strait of Hormuz crisis is the dominant macro force, with oil above $100, the IEA's record 400M barrel stockpile release, and ripple effects hitting markets (S&P at 2026 lows), energy, semiconductors, and cybersecurity. Stagflation risks are now front and center.

2. **Tesla Enters Chip Fabrication:** Musk's Terafab announcement — targeting 2nm chips at massive scale — signals a new phase of vertical integration in AI hardware. If successful, it would make Tesla the first major automaker with its own advanced chip foundry.

3. **AI Regulation Accelerates on Both Sides of the Atlantic:** The EU's AI Act amendment to ban AI-generated CSAM, 78 state-level chatbot bills in the US, and federal preemption deadlines all point to a rapidly tightening regulatory environment for AI, even as the Trump administration pushes for a more permissive federal framework.

4. **Robotics Funding Boom:** Six robotics companies becoming unicorns in February alone, plus $1B+ in robotics funding rounds this week (Mind Robotics, Rhoda AI), signals robotics may be entering its "ChatGPT moment" in terms of VC attention and capital deployment.

5. **Semiconductor Supply Chain Under Multi-Directional Stress:** The Nexperia-China dispute, Iran war's impact on chipmaking materials, BESI takeover interest, and China's renewed five-year plan push all highlight an industry caught between geopolitical crosscurrents and surging AI-driven demand.
