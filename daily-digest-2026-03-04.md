## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran War Reshapes Everything:** The U.S.-Israel military campaign against Iran is the dominant macro driver. The effective closure of the Strait of Hormuz threatens 15M barrels/day of oil supply, with Brent potentially reaching $100/barrel. Korean stocks suffered their worst crash since 2008. Defense stocks and oil are the clear beneficiaries; everything else faces headwinds from inflation fears and supply chain disruption.

2. **AI Capital Concentration Is Unprecedented:** Three companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, Waymo) absorbed 83% of all global VC in February. The $189B monthly record dwarfs historical norms and raises serious questions about capital allocation efficiency and bubble dynamics. Meanwhile, Nvidia is deploying billions into the photonics supply chain to address AI infrastructure bottlenecks.

3. **Anthropic's Defense Purge Creates Market Opening:** The Pentagon's designation of Anthropic as a supply-chain risk effectively hands the military AI market to OpenAI and Google. Defense contractors must now remove Anthropic's tools from their operations — a significant disruption that could reshape the competitive dynamics of the AI industry.

4. **Apple's M5 Pro/Max Bets Big on On-Device AI:** The new Fusion Architecture with Neural Accelerators positions Apple for the emerging local AI inference market. Up to 4x AI performance improvements could make MacBook Pro a viable platform for running large language models locally.

5. **Energy Infrastructure Is a War Target:** The attacks on Qatar's LNG facilities, Saudi refineries, and the Hormuz chokepoint demonstrate that energy infrastructure has become a primary target in modern conflict. Countries dependent on Middle East energy (Asia, Europe) face acute supply risk.

6. **Silicon Photonics Moment Has Arrived:** Nvidia's combined $4.5B investment in Coherent, Lumentum, and Ayar Labs within 24 hours signals that optical interconnects are transitioning from R&D to production infrastructure for AI data centers. The copper bottleneck is real.
