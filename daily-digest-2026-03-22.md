## AI & Machine Learning

### WordPress.com Opens Platform to AI Agents via MCP
- **Source:** TechCrunch, The Verge
- **URL:** https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/20/wordpress-com-now-lets-ai-agents-write-and-publish-posts-and-more/
- **Summary:** WordPress.com now allows AI agents like Claude and ChatGPT to draft, edit, and publish blog posts via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). The platform, which powers over 43% of all websites, will let agents manage comments, update metadata, and organize content. All agent-written posts start as drafts for human review. The move could fundamentally change how web content is created and maintained at scale.

### Meta Replacing Content Moderators with AI Systems
- **Source:** The Verge, Meta
- **URL:** https://about.fb.com/news/2026/03/boosting-your-support-and-safety-on-metas-apps-with-ai/
- **Summary:** Meta announced a wide rollout of its AI support assistant for Facebook and Instagram, stating it will "reduce reliance on third-party vendors" employing human content moderators over the next few years. The AI systems will handle repetitive reviews of graphic content and areas where adversarial actors constantly change tactics, such as illicit drug sales and scams.

### Signal Creator Moxie Marlinspike Working with Meta on Encrypted AI
- **Source:** The Verge, Confer Blog
- **URL:** https://confer.to/blog/2026/03/encrypted-meta/
- **Summary:** Moxie Marlinspike, creator of Signal messenger, announced he is working to integrate his encrypted AI chatbot Confer's privacy technology to underpin Meta AI. The collaboration could bring end-to-end encryption to Meta's AI assistant, addressing a major privacy concern in the AI chatbot space.

### FedEx Rolls Out Enterprise-Wide AI Literacy Training for 440,000 Workers
- **Source:** CNBC
- **URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/21/fedex-jobs-ai-training.html

The "living curriculum" includes personalized, role-based training designed to make employees more knowledgeable, efficient, and promotion-ready. - **Source:** BleepingComputer
- **URL:** https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/trivy-vulnerability-scanner-breach-pushed-infostealer-via-github-actions/

- **Source:** CNBC, IEA
- **URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iea-oil-fuel-prices-energy-advice-consumers-crisis.html
- **Summary:** The International Energy Agency warned that supply measures alone won't mitigate "the largest supply disruption in the history of the global oil market."
