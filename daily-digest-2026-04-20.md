## Markets & Finance

### Oil surges, equities and crypto wobble on renewed US–Iran tensions
- **Source:** CoinDesk / CoinCentral
- **URL:** https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/04/20/bitcoin-ether-solana-slide-oil-jumps-on-renewed-u-s-iran-war-risks

### IMF cuts 2026 global growth to 3.1% on Middle East war risk
- **Source:** IMF WEO April 2026
- **URL:** https://www.imf.org/en/publications/weo/issues/2026/04/14/world-economic-outlook-april-2026
- **Summary:** The IMF's April World Economic Outlook projects global growth slowing to 3.1% in 2026 and 3.2% in 2027, assuming the Middle East conflict remains limited in scope and duration. Hormuz flow remains the single most important variable for energy supply and the global economy.
- **Impact:** Base case is benign; tail risks from oil transit disruption are now explicit in Fund forecasts — positioning should reflect the left tail.
