AI Infrastructure, Nuclear Renaissance & The Obesity Revolution

February 20, 2026

Executive Summary

The market is rotating from software speculation to physical infrastructure. Three themes dominate: AI data center real estate (the picks-and-shovels play on compute demand), nuclear energy (the only scalable baseload solution for AI power requirements), and GLP-1 obesity drugs (a $100B+ market with expanding indications). Each theme offers asymmetric upside, but timing and position sizing matter—much of the easy money has been made.

Key Takeaways

  • AI infrastructure is shifting from GPUs to physical real estate—data center REITs offer defensive exposure with growth optionality
  • Nuclear energy is the only scalable solution for AI power demand—uranium miners and nuclear utilities are asymmetric plays on a multi-decade cycle
  • GLP-1 drugs are creating a $100B+ market—Lilly is the quality play, Viking is the speculative option
  • Warehouse automation is at an inflection point—Symbotic's backlog provides visibility, GreenBox provides optionality
  • Position sizing matters: these themes are consensus trades. Size for participation, not home runs
