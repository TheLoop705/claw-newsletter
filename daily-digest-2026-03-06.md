- **Source:** CNN, Reuters, TechCrunch, AP
- **URL:** https://edition.cnn.com/2026/03/05/politics/fbi-investigating-cyber-breach-critical-surveillance-network
- **Summary:** The FBI disclosed it is investigating "suspicious activities" on a sensitive internal network used to manage wiretaps and intelligence surveillance warrants. ### 'Coruna' iPhone Exploit Kit — A Possible "EternalBlue Moment" for Mobile
- **Source:** WIRED, The Hacker News, CyberScoop
- **URL:** https://www.wired.com/story/coruna-iphone-hacking-toolkit-us-government/
- **Summary:** Google identified a "new and powerful" iOS exploit kit dubbed "Coruna" (aka CryptoWaters) targeting iPhones running iOS 13 through 17.2.1, using 23 exploits across five attack chains. Security researchers believe it was likely developed by the U.S. government and leaked, drawing comparisons to the NSA's EternalBlue. It has been linked to Russian espionage and mass-scale attacks. Apple patched the vulnerabilities in iOS 26, and Lockdown Mode blocks it.
