## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran Conflict Reshaping Markets:** The Strait of Hormuz crisis is the dominant macro force — oil at $80-85/bbl, capital rotating from tech to energy/utilities/materials, and inflation fears resurfacing. Duration of conflict is the key variable for global economic impact.

2. **AI Power Struggle Intensifies:** The Anthropic-Pentagon blacklist and OpenAI's GitHub rival both signal an increasingly competitive and politically charged AI landscape. Companies must navigate both commercial rivalry and government alignment.

3. **Venture Capital Concentration Risk:** $189B in VC in a single month is staggering, but 83% going to three companies is unprecedented concentration. The gap between AI mega-rounds and the broader startup ecosystem continues to widen.

4. **Semiconductor Geopolitics Accelerating:** Four Chinese foundries in the global top 10, India courting ASML suppliers, and SMIC's $5.6B consolidation deal all point to an accelerating realignment of the global chip supply chain.

5. **Cybersecurity at Inflection Point:** Iran cyber threats escalating just as CISA faces shutdowns and furloughs creates a dangerous gap. Meanwhile, AI is turbocharging both attacks and basic security gap exploitation.
