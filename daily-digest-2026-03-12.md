## Investment Themes & Takeaways

1. **Iran War Dominates Everything:** The US-Iran conflict is the macro story driving markets, energy, semiconductors, and geopolitics simultaneously. The 172M-barrel SPR release signals Washington acknowledges the price pain but may not be enough if Strait of Hormuz disruptions escalate.

2. **AI Industry Fractures Along Political Lines:** The Trump-Anthropic feud is unprecedented — a sitting president banning an AI company from government use over perceived political bias. This creates clear winners (OpenAI, Google, xAI) and losers (Anthropic) in the government AI market.

3. **Nvidia GTC Week Is Make-or-Break:** With Vera Rubin and Feynman architectures expected, next week's GTC could either justify Nvidia's valuation or confirm that custom silicon (Broadcom/TPU) is eating into its dominance.

4. **Custom Silicon War Heating Up:** Anthropic's $21B Broadcom TPU order, combined with OpenAI's custom chip ambitions, signals a structural shift away from GPU-only AI infrastructure toward purpose-built silicon.

5. **Apple's Quiet Hardware Refresh:** The M5 Pro/Max launch, MacBook Neo, and iPhone 17e represent Apple's largest simultaneous product launch in years — yet it's barely making headlines against the geopolitical backdrop.

6. **Megadeal Cycle Accelerates:** With 22 billion-dollar deals in 10 weeks and PE confidence at a six-year high, 2026 is shaping up as a landmark year for M&A — if geopolitical risks don't derail it.
