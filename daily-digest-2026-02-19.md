## AI & Machine Learning

### The Rise of "Theorizer" and Scientific Theory Synthesis
- **Source:** Boston Institute of Analytics
- **URL:** https://bostoninstituteofanalytics.org/blog/latest-machine-learning-updates-in-2026-key-developments-in-generative-ai-this-week-2nd-6th-feb/
- **Summary:** The Allen Institute for AI (Ai2) introduced **Theorizer** on February 2nd, a system that reads scientific literature and writes testable theories directly. The system achieved 0.88-0.90 precision on benchmark assessments testing nearly 3,000 laws. This represents a shift from "chatty" assistants to "knowledge compressors" for research fields like pharmacology and materials science.

### Agentic AI: From Chatbots to Operating Systems
- **Source:** Boston Institute of Analytics
- **URL:** https://bostoninstituteofanalytics.org/blog/latest-machine-learning-updates-in-2026-key-developments-in-generative-ai-this-week-2nd-6th-feb/
- **Summary:** The transition from the "Chatbot" era to the "Agentic" era is accelerating. Amdocs introduced AOS (Agentic Operating System) for telecommunications on February 5th, enabling AI agents to perform independent tasks across business processes including fraud detection and supply chain management. Vercel AI Accelerator updates demonstrate AI agents can now make critical architecture decisions within codebases.

### Anthropic Study: AI-Assisted Learning Shows Lower Mastery
- **Source:** Boston Institute of Analytics / Anthropic
- **URL:** https://bostoninstituteofanalytics.org/blog/latest-machine-learning-updates-in-2026-key-developments-in-generative-ai-this-week-2nd-6th-feb/
- **Summary:** A February 2nd Anthropic study found software engineers using AI tools completed tasks faster but achieved only 50% mastery on concept quizzes versus 67% for the manual group. This raises questions about "learning by prompting" versus deep understanding in AI-assisted education.

### Meta to Deploy Millions of Nvidia AI Chips
- **Source:** CNBC
- **URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/17/stock-market-today-live-updates.html
- **Summary:** Meta announced plans to use millions of Nvidia chips in its data center buildout, driving Nvidia shares up more than 2%. This signals continued massive infrastructure investment by hyperscalers despite investor concerns about AI spending returns.

- **Source:** Tech Policy Press

### AI Shifting Power from Governments to Tech Companies
- **Source:** Rest of World
