Executive Summary

This issue examines the critical infrastructure layer of semiconductor manufacturing. EUV lithography remains the ultimate bottleneck, with ASML and TSMC commanding irreplaceable positions. Process control leader KLA offers defensive quality, while Intel presents a high-risk turnaround opportunity and AMD continues its data center momentum.

Key Takeaways

  • 1. EUV lithography is the most critical chokepoint in advanced semiconductor manufacturing
  • 2. ASML and TSMC have built irreplaceable moats through decades of R&D and capital investment
  • 3. Process control intensity increases exponentially at each new node, benefiting KLA
  • 4. Intel's foundry pivot is a binary outcome - significant upside if execution succeeds
  • 5. AMD remains the best risk/reward in AI compute outside of NVIDIA
