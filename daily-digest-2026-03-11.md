1. **Iran War Reshapes Everything:** The U.S.-Israel war with Iran is the dominant macro force — oil's 50%+ YTD surge, defense spending, supply chain disruptions, and cyber warfare all trace back to this conflict. Watch for de-escalation signals as the key market catalyst.

The "foundation model lab" category is now a distinct asset class attracting billion-dollar seed rounds.

3. **CPI + Fed = Volatility Ahead:** Today's CPI release and next week's Fed meeting are the next major market-moving events. With oil prices elevated and labor data revised down, the Fed faces a difficult balancing act between inflation and recession risk.

4. **Nuclear Renaissance:** The Paris Nuclear Energy Summit signals a major policy shift, with EU leaders calling the nuclear retreat a "strategic mistake." Nuclear energy companies (EDF, Constellation, Cameco) may benefit from renewed government support.

5. **Semiconductor $1T Milestone Under Threat:** While chips are on track for a record $1 trillion market in 2026, the Iran conflict, memory shortages, and geopolitical tensions could derail the trajectory. TSMC's below-forecast growth is an early warning sign.
